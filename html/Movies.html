Avengers Movies

The Avengers is a superhero film franchise based on the Marvel Comics superhero team of the same name. The films follow the adventures of a group of superheroes, including Iron Man, Thor, Captain America, Black Widow, the Hulk, and Hawkeye, as they come together to protect the world from various threats.

The franchise began with the 2012 film, The Avengers, which was directed by Joss Whedon and starred a star-studded cast. Since then, the franchise has continued with several sequels, each introducing new characters and featuring epic battles against powerful villains.

The storylines of the films are based on the original Avengers comic book series, which was first published in 1963. The comics have been beloved by fans for decades, and the films have brought these iconic characters to a new generation of viewers.
